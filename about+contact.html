About the creator...

My name is Mel Lovelace-Dodoo. I have always been a person who is captivated by flaws. Being queer, half-African, and half-Filipino, I have encountered many situations where I was told to be a certain way and keep myself limited to whichever side of my cultural background people would rather identify me with. I stand as an individual whose passions is in creating loud cartoons, and I am set to achieve my dream as becoming a part of the animation industry as a storyboard artist.

My passion to create was the driving force behind my work, and by senior year I was asked to assist my teacher in teaching the first animation class at my high school. From 2009-2010, I taught students from grades ten to twelve the basics of animation in Adobe Flash.

I have always seen myself as an individual who thrives under pressure. I have been cultivating the professional side of my artistic identity since the end of high school. In 2013, I applied to an art community named T-rumblr, a place where artists come together and share their work with others. There is a significant highlight on intensive competition within the community, but I took this environment as a challenge. Being a self-motivated individual, I strived to push myself creatively, connect with other artists, and produce work I hoped would be entertaining for not only me, but for others. After the first year, my work was noticed and I was promoted to the role of the community’s main artist moderator. My duty was set in creating multiple comic pages within short deadlines to start off news updates.

With this webcomic, I plan to teach about life hacks and make life easier for others through my art.
